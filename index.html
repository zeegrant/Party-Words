Party Words! A Hangman Type Game

Help Zuri get to the party with all her balloons by guessing the words correctly!

Zuri is tasked with bringing balloons to her friend’s party. Make sure she gets there safely by completing all of the party item words correctly. Start with 3 balloons and for every incorrect guess, Zuri loses a balloon. Zero balloons left means she can’t go to the party!
